# Understanding Checks: How This Learning Experience Works

## Check Questions
1. Can the learner describe how this learning experience works in their own words?
2. Do they understand that the pace and depth adapt to them?
3. Are they comfortable asking questions and interrupting when something is unclear?
4. Do they know where to find concept files and their own progress?

## Completion Criteria
- The learner understands the paradigm: interactive discussion + building, not lectures
- The learner knows the repo has concept files, wisdom files, and skills they can browse
- The learner is comfortable with the format and ready to proceed
- No signs of confusion about what to expect

## What to Update
- `learner/progress.md` — mark "How This Learning Experience Works" complete
- `learner/notes.md` — add observations about comfort level, initial impressions, any concerns raised
